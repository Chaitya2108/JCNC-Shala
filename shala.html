Our Mission

Our mission is to instill spiritual development, self-awareness, moral consciousness, social well-being, and Jain identity in individuals, based on the principles of Jainism.

Our Objective:

  • Bring happiness to our life through the proud heritage of Jainism
  • Enhance our physical, mental, emotional and spiritual health while maintaining fundamental values of Jainism
  • Adhere to the highest standards of ethics and integrity
  • Create positive environment around teachers, participants, family members and friends
  • Utilize latest scientific information where applicable

Shala Volunteering & Donations

JCNC runs on donations and volunteering from our members. In order to provide all the shala members as successful an experience as possible, we humbly ask all of you to donate and volunteer in whichever capacity possible. Below, you can find additional information.

  • Volunteer for Classes: The education Department is looking for volunteers in areas of Teachers, Coordinators, Kitchen Leads. Specifically, we have openings for Music and Dance Teachers, Choreographers. If you are interested in teaching/volunteering please fill this form. Please visit this link to learn more about the positions available. to learn more about the positions available. Please email education@jcnc.org if you have any more questions.
  • Donations: Please support the Education Department with your Financial Tax Deductible donations. You can Donate at Link below and select education from the drop-down menu. Or drop off a Check in the JCNC Finance Mail Box. Please select Education while donating at the link below or Checks should be made out to JCNC and mention Education in the memo field of the check. Please visit http://www.jcnc.org/donate to donate. Please email education@jcnc.org if you have any more questions.
  • Membership: JCNC Membership is based on Calendar year. You can signup or renew you membership online at http://www.jcnc.org/. Please email pr@jcnc.org if you have any questions.
  • Our library: JCNC Library has a wide selection of books in English, Hindi and Gujarati. The library collection spans from Agams to children's books and everything in between. The audio visual collection of the library contains bhajans, various videos, and recorded discourses. JCNC Library is located on the ground floor of the Jain Center. The library is open concurrent to the Jain Shala, Sundays from 10:00 AM - Noon. JCNC Members can check-out books and audio visual content from the library except the reference books such as Agams. If you would like to volunteer for the library or make a contribution or donate Jain Studies books, please email library@jcnc.org.
